Cyclone alerts and survival steps for Odisha — hold on till help arrives

⚠ CYCLONE WARNING — Coastal Odisha — Landfall expected in 6 hours

Before, during, and after

Before landfall

  • Move to the nearest cyclone shelter if advised
  • Store 3 days of drinking water and dry food
  • Charge phones and power banks fully
  • Tie down loose roofing and outdoor items

During the storm

  • Stay indoors, away from windows
  • Do not go out even if the wind pauses briefly
  • Avoid using landline phones during lightning
  • Keep the emergency bag within reach

After the storm

  • Avoid fallen power lines and flooded roads
  • Boil or treat water before drinking
  • Check on elderly and injured neighbours
  • Wait for an official all-clear before returning home

Surviving till help arrives

If you are stranded

  1. Move to the highest, most stable point nearby
  2. Ration water — small sips, not large gulps
  3. Signal with bright cloth, a mirror, or a torch in short bursts
  4. Stay together as a group — do not wander off alone
  5. Conserve phone battery for one call, not constant checking
